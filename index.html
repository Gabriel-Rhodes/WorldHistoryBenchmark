U.S. Wealth Inequality Over The Last Century

Wealth Inequality is a Phrase That's Becomming Increasingly Popular. But What is it?

   The definition I will be using for the term wealth inequality is differences in the distribution of wealth. The United States has a long history of changes in wealth inequality and distribution. So what exactly will be covered on this site?

   First, we will look at statistics regarding wealth inequality in the United States over the last century. These statistics will include how wealth inequality has varied over time, who is most affected by wealth inequality, and what economic changes occurred around key points in wealth inequality history.

   Next, we’ll look at how wealth inequality affects individual’s lives, and understand more of the impact it has. Lastly, we’ll see how people are talking about wealth inequality, what’s being done about it, and what changes these people are aiming for.
