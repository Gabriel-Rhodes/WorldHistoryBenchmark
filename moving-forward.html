How Are People Dealing With Wealth Inequality?

So What if Wealth Inequality is Something That Affects Society? What are People Doing About it?

Wealth Inequality has grown in the U.S. over the last 40 years. But what is being done about it? For one, as a society, we are talking more about it. According to a Google NGram, wealth inequality as a phrase has grown in use over eightyfold in the last 40 years. As a country and a globe, we are talking more about wealth inequality. This allows us to come up with ideas that can help ease issues that we have as a society. Politicians are starting to talk more about this idea of wealth inequality, and more voters are developing more complicated opinions about wealth inequality.

Ultimately, what Americans are doing about wealth inequality is they are talking about it. They are sharing ideas, electing leaders with their own ideas on it, and in general writing, reading, and learning about it. The best way to solve issue such as these are to first start by formulating opinions and ideas for how to solve them.
